# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - DEV & Design Labs

## 1. Executive Summary
DEV & Design Labs is a premier "proof-of-work" network specifically designed for **students and graduates** of the **Dev and Design Academy**. It serves as a launchpad where talented individuals and teams showcase their real-world projects, secure sponsorships, and connect with recruiters seeking proven builders.

## 2. Vision & Mission
### 2.1 Vision
To flip the traditional hiring model by prioritizing *shipped products* over resumes. We aim to be the definitive ecosystem where the next generation of tech talent is discovered through their tangible contributions to innovation.

### 2.3 Mission
To provide a platform for Dev and Design Academy students and graduates to collaborate, innovate, and broadcast their technical and creative prowess to a global audience of sponsors and talent hunters.

## 3. Core Features
### 3.1 Project Showcase (The Lab)
- **Team-Based Innovation:** Emphasis on collaborative projects built by student teams.
- **Proof-of-Work:** Verified project links (GitHub, Live Demos) and technical documentation.
- **Discovery Feed:** A curated stream of the latest and most trending builds.

### 3.2 Sponsorship & Funding
- **Sponsor Hub:** A space for patrons, DAOs, and companies to discover and fund promising student projects.
- **Grant Programs:** Structured funding opportunities for specific categories (AI, Web3, Fintech, etc.).

### 3.3 Recruitment (Hunter Hub)
- **Talent Acquisition:** Recruiters can browse projects to find builders with specific skill sets.
- **Direct Hiring:** Streamlined connection between top-tier talent and industry leaders.

### 3.4 Academy Integration
- **Student Verification:** Integration with Dev and Design Academy student IDs and certificates.
- **Alumni Network:** Continued access for graduates to showcase their professional evolution.

## 4. Design Philosophy
- **Aesthetic:** Mimicking the high-end, polished feel of `devdesignhq.com`.
- **Typography:** Bold, display-oriented fonts (Plus Jakarta Sans) paired with clean body text (Inter).
- **Interactive:** Premium shadows, glassmorphism, and subtle micro-interactions to enhance the "builder" vibe.

## 5. Technical Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18+, Vite, Tailwind CSS 4.0.
- **Animations:** Framer Motion (motion/react).
- **Icons:** Lucide React.
- **Styling:** Utility-first CSS with custom design tokens.
